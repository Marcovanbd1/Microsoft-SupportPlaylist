Privacy and trust

This only works if people trust it

Support Playlists handle the most sensitive moments in people's lives.

Privacy is not a feature. It is the foundation.

What Microsoft commits to

  • No personal data stored without explicit consent
  • No conversations sold to advertisers
  • No tracking of who accesses what support
  • Local processing where possible
  • End-to-end encryption
  • Right to delete everything
  • Full transparency in data use

What people control

  • Anonymous mode option
  • Location sharing optional
  • Save playlists locally only
  • Share with trusted contacts
  • Clear history anytime
  • Opt out completely

This is not surveillance.
This is not data extraction.
This is care.

Trust through action: Microsoft partners with established support organizations, crisis lines, and public health bodies. Local services verify their listings. Communities have oversight. This is built with people, not just for them.
